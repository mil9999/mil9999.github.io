More than just data.

I grew up in Nepal, completed my Bachelor of Computer Applications in Dehradun, India, and then moved to France to pursue my MSc in Business Analytics at NEOMA Business School — a journey across three countries that shaped how I see the world and approach problems.

I'm drawn to data because it tells stories that people often miss. Whether it's uncovering a -0.98 ESG-profitability correlation in automotive companies or building a GenAI pipeline that turns noisy hotel reviews into structured intelligence, I care about turning raw information into real decisions.

Outside of data, I'm a passionate footballer — I represented my college in multiple competitions and have played throughout my life. I'm also an avid trekker: I've completed trails to Annapurna Base Camp, Phoksundo Lake, Rara Tal, and Kedarnath. I'm currently planning to tackle the Mont Blanc trail and other routes across Europe.

I believe in giving back to communities. During my college days in Dehradun, I helped organise a blind cricket tournament — one of the most humbling and rewarding experiences of my life. It taught me that great outcomes come from listening, adapting, and leading with empathy.
